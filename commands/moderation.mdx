Gacky-X provides a full suite of moderation commands with built-in role hierarchy protection and automatic logging.

## Standard Punishments

<ResponseField name="-ban <@user|ID> [reason]" type="Command">
  Permanently ban a user.
  - **Example:** `-ban @Scammer malicious intent`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="-kick <@user|ID> [reason]" type="Command">
  Remove a user from the server.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="-mute <@user|ID> [duration] [reason]" type="Command">
  Timeout a user. Supports dynamic durations.
  - **Example:** `-mute @User 1h stop spamming`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="-warn <@user|ID> [reason]" type="Command">
  Issue a formal warning to a user.
</ResponseField>

## Bulk Actions

### Purge (`-purge`)
Bulk delete messages with advanced filtering.
- **Usage:** `-purge <amount> [@user] [bot|link|embed]`
- **Example:** `-purge 50 @User` (Deletes 50 messages from a specific user).

### Mass Role (`-role`)
Manage roles for entire groups of users.
- **Usage:** `-role <humans|bots|everyone> <add|remove> @Role`
- **Warning:** High-frequency role actions are queued to prevent rate limits.

## Dynamic Durations

Commands that support time (like `-mute` and `-lock`) accept the following formats:
- `s`: Seconds
- `m`: Minutes
- `h`: Hours
- `d`: Days (Max 28 days for timeouts)

**Example:** `-timeout @User 2d 12h`
