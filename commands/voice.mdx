Gacky-X provides staff with high-precision controls for voice communication.

## Voice Dashboard (`-voice`)

Trigger the **Voice Control Center** to manage everyone in your current channel instantly.

### Channel Actions
- **Mute All:** Server-mutes every member in the channel.
- **Deafen All:** Server-deafens every member in the channel.
- **Kick Everyone:** Disconnects everyone from the channel.
- **Lock Channel:** Prevents anyone from joining.
- **Hide Channel:** Makes the channel invisible to regular members.

### Mass Movement
- **`-voice moveall <#target-channel>`**: Drags everyone from your current voice room to another specific channel.

<Tip>
  **Permissions:** You must be in a voice channel to use the dashboard controls, and Gacky-X must have the **Move Members** and **Mute Members** permissions.
</Tip>
