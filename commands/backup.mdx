Gacky-X allows you to create full snapshots of your server's configuration for emergency recovery.

## Purpose

The Backup system saves:
*   Channel names, types, and categories.
*   Role names, colors, and permissions.
*   Category structures.

## Commands

### Create Backup (`-backup create`)
Generates a unique **Backup ID**. 
- **Storage:** Data is encrypted and stored in our secure database.
- **Note:** Large servers may take a few seconds to process.

### View Archives (`-backup list`)
Displays a list of all saved snapshots for your server with their IDs and timestamps.

### Restore Snapshot (`-backup restore <Backup-ID>`)
**CRITICAL:** Restores the configuration from the specified snapshot.

<Warning>
  **Protocol Restriction:** Restoring a backup requires the **Administrator** permission. We recommend performing restores during low-activity hours as the bot will recreate many channels and roles.
</Warning>

## Important Notes

*   **Role Hierarchy:** Gacky-X can only restore roles that are **below** its own highest role.
*   **Member Data:** Backups do **not** store individual member messages or private data; they focus on server structure and security.
