Gacky-X combines high-speed regex filters with cutting-edge AI to provide a multi-layered chat defense.

## The Traditional Filter (Fast & Free)

The standard AutoMod (`-automod`) is your first line of defense. It handles:
- **Spam:** Rapid-fire messages.
- **Links:** Phishing and invite links.
- **Bad Words:** Toxic language.

These filters are **Instant** and do not cost any tokens.

## The AI Shield (Smart & Deep)

Standard filters are easily bypassed by using "leetspeak" or clever spacing. The AI Moderation (`-aimod`) understands **Context**.

### What the AI Detects:
- **Token Grabbers:** Malicious scripts hidden in messages.
- **Coordinated Raids:** "Ghost" users joining to cause chaos.
- **Advanced Toxicity:** Harassment that doesn't use obvious swear words.

### Recommended AI Strategy:
1.  **Start Flag-Only:** Run `-aimod` and set the action to **Flag**. Watch the alerts in your `security-logs` for 24 hours.
2.  **Fine-Tune Confidence:** If the AI is too sensitive, increase the **Min Confidence** to `85%`.
3.  **Activate Deletion:** Once you trust the AI's logic, set the action to **Delete** to clean chat automatically.

<Warning>
  **Privacy Note:** AI moderation only scans the content of the message. It never collects user emails, IP addresses, or private tokens.
</Warning>
