Welcome to the Gacky-X community! Follow this guide to turn your server from "Open" to "Fortified" in under 5 minutes.

## Step 1: Baseline Permissions
After inviting the bot, your first priority is the **Role Hierarchy**.
1.  Go to **Server Settings > Roles**.
2.  Find the **Gacky-X** role.
3.  Drag it to the **very top** (or as high as possible).
4.  This ensures Gacky-X can moderate your staff and protect your highest roles.

## Step 2: Surveillance Deployment
You cannot secure what you cannot see. Initialize your log channels immediately:
1.  Type **`-setup`**.
2.  Click **Initialize Logs** (or run `-autologs`).
3.  This creates the `GACKY LOGS` category. Your audit trail is now live.

## Step 3: Perimeter Defense
Enable the core Antinuke protection to prevent rogue admins from damaging the server.
1.  Type **`-antinuke`**.
2.  Click **Enable** on the **Main Shield**.
3.  Set your thresholds using **`-thresholds`** (we recommend starting with the default "Medium" values).

## Step 4: Chat Purification
Setup Automod to handle spam and toxic content automatically.
1.  Type **`-automod`**.
2.  Enable **Anti-Spam**, **Anti-Link**, and **Anti-Invite**.
3.  Set the punishment to `TIMEOUT` for a safe start.

## Step 5: Verify Your Health
Finally, run the security scan to see if you missed anything:
```txt
-securityscore
```
If your score is below 80, check the "Failed Checks" section of the card and adjust your server settings accordingly.
